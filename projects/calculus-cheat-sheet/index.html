Derivatives

General Form

$$\displaystyle f'(x) = \lim\limits_{h \to \infty} \frac{f(x+h)- f(x)}{h}$$

General

$$f(x)$$ $$f'(x)$$
$$x^n$$ $$nx^{n-1}$$
$$f(g(x))$$ $$f'(g(x))\cdot g'(x)$$
$$\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}$$ $$\frac{f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x)}{g^2(x)}$$
$$f(x)\cdot g(x)$$ $$f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x)$$
